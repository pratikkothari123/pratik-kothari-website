Tax Effectiveness Scores

Schwab, C. M., Stomberg, B., & Williams, B. M. (2022). Effective tax planning. The Accounting Review, 97(1), 413–437.

1 Year Scores (xlsx) 5 Year Scores (xlsx) SSW 2022

This dataset uses the sample selection criteria of Schwab, Stomberg, and Williams (2022) over the period 1994–2024. Tax haven affiliate data for fiscal years 2020 and beyond are obtained from the OpenSanctions US CorpWatch dataset. The resulting scores exhibit an approximately 92.9% correlation with those independently maintained on Brian Williams' website. Last updated: June 2026.
